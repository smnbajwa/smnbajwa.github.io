FEA of an idler pulley of an automobile

Home

Date added: 09 November 2022

Status: Finished

Summary:

Idler pulley are the engine pulleys that are responsible for guiding and tension among the engine drive belts.

The aim of this project was to model the stresses and strains on an idler pulley using ANSYS Workbench

The angle of wrap by the belt is 180°. Because of the symmetry of loading, we used half section for our analysis. This results in a finer mesh as the total volume of the pulley is reduced.

We were limited by the ANSYS student license to a maximum numbers of elements.

Data:

Nodes 28898
Elements 5600
Material AISI 1008 Steel
Young's modulus 210 Gpa
Poisson's ratio 0.29
Bulk modulus 166 Gpa
Shear modulus 81 Gpa
Tensile yield strength 135 Mpa
